Why I built this

"When I'm reciting and my brain slips from one verse to a similar one, I want a tool that captures that relationship — so next time I encounter it, I can immediately recall: ah, this is the one that sounds like 60:9. The difference is here."

Mutashabihat — verses that are nearly identical in wording — are one of the hardest parts of Quran memorization. Standard review tools don't model this kind of confusion. hifzlink does.

60:8 · Al-Mumtahanah

لَّا يَنْهَاكُمُ اللَّهُ عَنِ الَّذِينَ لَمْ يُقَاتِلُوكُمْ فِي الدِّينِ...

{{/* ── How it works ───────────────────────────────────────── */}}

How it works

Three steps from confusion to clarity

  1. 1

    Encounter a confusion

    During recitation, your mind slips — you land on the wrong verse or can't tell two similar ones apart.

  2. 2

    Search for the pair

    Type the ayah reference into Search. If the pair is saved, you'll see it immediately — along with any other verses that share the same confusion pattern.

  3. 3

    Compare and lock it in

    Open the side-by-side compare view. The highlighted diff shows exactly which words differ — so your brain builds the right anchor.
